What I Offer

Hoof Care Built Around Your Horse

Every horse is different. I tailor every visit to what your horse actually needs — no shortcuts, no guesswork.

🔨

Horseshoeing

Custom shoeing for performance, protection, and discipline-specific needs. Hot or cold shoeing available.

✂️

Hoof Trimming

Balanced trims to maintain natural hoof health and support proper movement — every 6–8 weeks.

🩺

Corrective Shoeing

Targeted solutions for lameness, imbalance, and structural issues to restore comfort and soundness.

🌿

Barefoot Trimming

Natural barefoot trimming for horses that perform best without shoes — with careful attention to balance.
