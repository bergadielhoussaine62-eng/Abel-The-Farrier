What We Offer

Farrier Services

Comprehensive hoof care for horses of all ages, breeds, and needs — in Monument, Colorado Springs, and surrounding areas.

✂️

Hoof Trimming

Regular hoof trimming is one of the most important things you can do for your horse's long-term health. Mike provides precise, balanced trims that encourage correct hoof growth and prevent painful cracks and splits.

We work with horses of all breeds and temperaments, assessing each hoof individually rather than applying a one-size-fits-all approach.

🔨

Shoeing

We offer a full range of shoeing options:

  • Conventional shoeing — standard steel or aluminium shoes for everyday use and performance
  • Therapeutic shoeing — specialized shoes for horses with soundness issues, navicular syndrome, or laminitis
  • Custom solutions — Mike fabricates and adapts shoes to match your horse's individual hoof shape
🩹

Hoof Rehabilitation

If your horse has experienced hoof injuries or chronic lameness, we offer dedicated rehabilitation services aimed at restoring health and function over time.

Mike works closely with your veterinarian — including reviewing X-rays and coordinating on treatment plans — to make sure your horse gets the most consistent care possible.

🐎

All Ages & Breeds

From young horses just starting their hoof care journey to older horses with complex needs, Mike has experience with a wide range of ages and breeds — including draft breeds like Shires.

His calm, quiet approach keeps horses relaxed and makes the experience easier for both the horse and the owner.

Not sure what your horse needs?

Reach out and Mike will be happy to talk through what's best for your horse before booking.
